Sachcha Prayas is committed to creating a society where every citizen, especially women and children, can lead a life of dignity and equality. Their mission encompasses various initiatives to promote income generation activities, environmental security, health and hygiene practices, legal awareness, and child rights. They aim to promote environmental sustainability, social justice, and support those in need. Through their various awareness campaigns, they hope to create mass awareness among community members and foster a people-centered approach to advocacy on common issues.
