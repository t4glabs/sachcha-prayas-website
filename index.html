Sachcha Prayas is committed to creating a society where every citizen, especially women and children, can lead a life of dignity and equality. Their mission encompasses various initiatives to promote income generation activities, environmental security, health and hygiene practices, legal awareness, and child rights. They aim to promote environmental sustainability, social justice, and support those in need. Through their various awareness campaigns, they hope to create mass awareness among community members and foster a people-centered approach to advocacy on common issues.

Mission

To empower the marginalised section of society especially women and children and ensure their reach to access knowledge and information which helps to lead a dignified life.

Vision

To create a society where every citizen, especially women and children, lives with dignity and has equal access to services related to health, education, and other basic needs services.

Objective

This project aims to empower women and girls by promoting income generation skills, raising awareness, and improving access to rights. It also focuses on environmental security, health, and legal awareness to build a sustainable and empowered community.
